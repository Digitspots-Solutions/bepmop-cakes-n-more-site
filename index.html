About Nulis Edu KYC Technology Limited

Nulis Edu KYC Technology Limited (also operating as Nulis Edu RegTech Nigeria) is a pioneering fintech company specializing in Know Your Customer (KYC) verification and regulatory technology solutions. Based in Karu, Nasarawa State, we serve financial institutions, insurance companies, and businesses across Nigeria.

Founded by an experienced InsurTech professional, we understand the unique challenges Nigerian businesses face in meeting regulatory requirements while maintaining operational efficiency. Our technology-driven approach transforms compliance from a burden into a competitive advantage.

We combine local expertise with international best practices to deliver KYC and compliance solutions that are robust, scalable, and tailored to the Nigerian regulatory environment. Our mission is to make compliance accessible, affordable, and efficient for businesses of all sizes.

Comprehensive RegTech Solutions

KYC Verification Services

Streamlined identity verification processes that meet CBN and regulatory standards. We verify customer identities quickly and accurately using advanced biometric technology, database checks, and document verification systems.

Compliance Technology Solutions

End-to-end compliance management platforms designed for Nigerian financial institutions. Our solutions cover AML monitoring, transaction screening, and regulatory reporting, ensuring you stay ahead of compliance requirements.

InsurTech Consulting

Specialized consulting services for insurance companies navigating digital transformation and regulatory compliance. We help insurers implement technology solutions that improve customer onboarding, claims processing, and risk assessment.

Regulatory Advisory

Expert guidance on Nigerian financial regulations, including CBN directives, NAICOM requirements, and data protection laws. We keep your business informed and compliant as regulations evolve.

API Integration Services

Seamless integration of our KYC and compliance solutions into your existing systems. Our robust APIs enable real-time verification and compliance checks without disrupting your operations.

Training & Capacity Building

Comprehensive training programs for your compliance teams. We equip your staff with the knowledge and skills to effectively use compliance technology and understand regulatory requirements.
