About Bepmop Cakes N More

Established in the heart of Karu, Bepmop Cakes N More is a leading name in cake manufacturing and custom confectionery. We pride ourselves on using only the finest ingredients to create cakes that not only look stunning but taste exceptional.

Our experienced bakers understand the Nigerian palate and specialize in both traditional and contemporary designs. From small chops for your events to elaborate celebration cakes, we handle every order with meticulous attention to detail.

What sets us apart is our commitment to hygiene, punctual delivery, and customer satisfaction. We've become the go-to bakery for families, businesses, and event planners across Karu Local Government and beyond.

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I place my order?

We recommend ordering at least 48-72 hours in advance for custom cakes. For simple designs or our regular menu items, 24 hours notice is usually sufficient. For large events or elaborate wedding cakes, please contact us at least 2 weeks ahead.

Do you deliver to my area?

We deliver across Karu Local Government including Nyanya, Mararaba, New Karu, and surrounding areas. Delivery fees vary by location. Please call us to confirm delivery to your specific address.

Can I see my cake design before you make it?

Yes! We provide design consultations and can show you reference images or sketches based on your requirements. For wedding cakes, we offer tasting sessions as well.

What flavors do you offer?

We offer chocolate, vanilla, red velvet, marble, lemon, coconut, banana, and fruit cake. We can also create custom flavors upon request. All our cakes can be filled with buttercream, chocolate ganache, or fruit preserves.

Do you cater to dietary restrictions?

Yes, we can prepare sugar-free cakes and accommodate certain dietary needs. Please inform us when placing your order so we can advise on available options.

What are your payment options?

We accept cash, bank transfers, and mobile payments (Opay, PalmPay). A 50% deposit is required for custom cake orders, with the balance due on delivery.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 48 hours before delivery receive a full refund minus the deposit. Cancellations within 24 hours may forfeit the deposit as ingredients have been purchased.
